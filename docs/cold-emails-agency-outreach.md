## Testing notes

- **Start with Email 3** — shortest, question opener triggers self-check, risk reversal removes friction
- **Email 2 as second variant** — tests authority/proof framing vs. curiosity framing
- Kill variants below 3% reply rate after 100+ sends
- Iterate winners, don't guess — let reply rate data decide
- Personalize [Name] and [Agency] — keep everything else as-is until you have data
